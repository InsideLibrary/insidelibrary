Book Review: It Ends With Us

Title:

It Ends With Us

Author:

Colleen Hoover

Publication:

Atria books

Publication Year:

2 Aug 2016

Genre:

Romantic (Emotional)

Language:

English (Original Language)

About the Author

"It Ends with us this very famous book was penned down by "Colleen Hoover." She is an American author who primarily writes novels in the romance & young adult fiction generes & also she sometimes venture into the psychological thirller. Many of her works were self-published before being picked up by publishing house. As of October 2022 Hoover has sold approximately 20 million books. She is named one of the 100 most influential people in the world by Time Magazine in 2023. Her first book "slammed was published in November 2011 and "Ugly Love" is also very famous book Of her.

About the Book

"It Ends With Us" this book is very perfect example of the author's writing chops & her ability to weave together uplifting, romantic & somber plotliness. This book contains real life story of writer's mother & father & she also said that "this is the hardest book among her all compositions." Story of this book starts at a roof top of a building where our protagonist was sitting at a corner very lonely calmly, observing & of the sky and experiencing beauty of the nature, and story move forward from here taking lots of turns, creating eating curiosity in readers mind upto the end. This book is actually a novel kind of fiction which contains real love story of three normal people. It includes love triangle.

This novel has very normal story but die to writers beautiful, skillful and emotional discription and presentation of the story, goosebumps appears on readers body while reading this book. THis novel include love, romance, care and also domestic voilence speciality of this book is this book include lots of plots but writer is being able to separate them successfully to avoid confusion in readers mind.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Writer have use very simple sweet and straight forward language while composing this novel. As this books basic language is American English writers has also used some authentic beautiful and descriptive words from ancient American English. This a short novel with total 376 pages. This book contain 35 chapters each with new emotion.

My Opinion About the Book

I really like this book very much. I think this is really a fantanstic, mind-blowing book. Through this book readers can understand how to build relationships. I would like to suggest this book humbly to people over 16 because it also contains domestic voilance.
