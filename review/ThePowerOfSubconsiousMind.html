Book Review: The Power Of Subconscious Mind

Title:

The Power Of Subconscious Mind

Author:

Joseph Murphy

Publication:

Penguin Random House and JMW Group

Publication Year:

1963

Genre:

Self help (personal development)

Language:

English (Original)

About the Author

"The Power of Your Subconscious Mind" this groundbreaking book was written by one of the most renowned writers, "Sir Joseph Murphy." He came into the world on the 20th of May, 1898, and departed on the 16th of December, 1981. In his life of 83 years, he authored more than 30 best-selling books. He was not only a famous writer but also a highly regarded motivational speaker, personality developer, and great Irish philosopher. He conducted deep studies and made observations on the human brain and human psychology, introducing new philosophies related to the mind. His famous quote is "prayer is the soul's sincere desire."

About the Book

Have you wondered why someone is joyous while another is miserable, why someone is fearful and anxious while another exudes confidence, why so many good and kind people suffer the tortures of life? Dr. Joseph Murphy answers these questions in this book. He reveals the innate powers of the subconscious mind by fusing his spiritual wisdom and scientific research. This book proves to be one of the most inspirational guides of all time. It has helped millions across the globe achieve extraordinary goals and bring drastic improvements in their lives. And this, he says, can be done simply by changing the way one thinks. This book has unique powers to change people's attitudes optimistically.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

The writer has used very simple and straightforward language while composing this book. He also uses some wisdom to impart the beauty of the book. This book contains a total of twenty chapters, each with a separate summary to enrich readers with knowledge. This book is a beautiful example of a compact size with deep knowledge, as it has only 212 pages.

My Opinion About the Book

I think this book is really very useful for people in developing their personality. Packed with case studies of actual success stories, this book will help you unleash your mental powers, build confidence, create well-balanced friendships, improve marriages, amass wealth, conquer phobias, dispel bad habits, and even heal minor health ailments.
