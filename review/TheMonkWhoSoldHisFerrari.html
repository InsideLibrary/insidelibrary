Book Review: The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari

Title:

The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari

Authors:

Robin Sharma

Publisher:

Harper Collins

Publication Year:

1999

Genre:

Self help (personal development)

Language:

English (Original)

About the Authors

This ground breaking book was penned down by very famous writer "Robin Sharma." He is a canadian writer, best known for his "Monk Who Sold His Ferrari" book series Sharma worked as a litigation lawyer until age 25, when he self-published "Megaliving" in 1994 a book on "stress management and spirituality." He is also a founder of the training company "Sharma Leadership International." He mostly works on self help generes. He is also one of the great motivational speakers. He also have "The Brandlaureate Brand Personality Award" and "Golden Gavel" award.

About the Book

In this ground breaking book Sharma reveals the secrete behind the success of successful peaple. Through this book readers can get lots of values to improve their personality and quality of living. In this marvellous book Sharma dascribes the story of a millionaire who had all the materialistic things a middle class person can think of to live happily, still be had no satisfaction, Though having all the luxories that millonaire was unhappy and stressed but suddenly after meeting a monk that man's life take 360 degree turn he started living happily, satisfyingly and enjoing every second of his life. After meeting the monk that man's perspective changed optimistically. In this miraculous book author introduced his readers with the principal Of "seven element path" which leads the readers to success and prosperty. Through these book author wants to tell his readers some very important tips for beinging best player of their feild like "always give respect to time, don't miss opportunities always try to grab them, put your daily life into strict disciplined routen, defined your daily working hours, stay calm in every situation, doesn't matter how successful you became always stay humble and grounded." Through these book author explains the readers the way of quality life.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Author have used very simple, clear and straight forward language while composing this miraculous book. He also have used some proverbs & wisdoms for imparting beauty of language and for easy understanding of readers. Occassionly he also have used some complex words according to need of content. This book is a beautiful example of compact size with deep knowledge because it has only 196 pages.

My Opinion About the Book

I really like this book very much because this book has power to bring discipline in it's readers. Composing this book because though it has very simple language it gives readers very deep acknowledgements. It removes stress, frustration, anxiety from readers mind and fill their souls with positivity, confidence, happiness and kindness. I wowd like to suggest this book to every every human being.
