About the Author

One of the very fantastic books "Harry Potter and the philosophers Stone" is composed by one of the very famous story writers "J.K. Rowling." She is a british author who has authored a seven-volume fantasy series of Harry Potter published from 1997 to 2007. The series has sold over 600 million copies, & has been translated into 84 languages & spawned a global media franchise including films & video games. The "casual Vacancy (2012)" was her first novel for adults. She writes "crime cormoran strike", an ongoing "crime fiction" series, under the alias "Robert Galbraith."

About the Book

In this ground breaking book writer have use best of her imagination. This book has the power to hold the readers from first word to end of the book with same excitement. This is imaginary type of book filled with lots of curiosity, this book includes magical world, magicians, magic sticks, magic schools, the books is also paked with comic lines, and funny events also included in this book. This book contains lots of thriller and every new chapter come with new curiosity and new tension story of the book starts at a old house receiving letter and story beautifully moves forward introducing different characters and places speciality of these book is in this writers also have given importance to animals and trees. It also include mystery, friendship, games.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Writer have use very simple language while composing this book and also have use official language where it is needed. Writer have perfectely separated different plots in the story to avoid confusion. It has total 224 pages and included total 17 chapters.

My Opinion About the Book

I think this book is really very beautiful, impressive & miraculous. I think everyone should read this book because through this book readers can understand bravery, good and true friendship, and also get to know that bad and good both types of people exist in this world and we should be always aware of that.
