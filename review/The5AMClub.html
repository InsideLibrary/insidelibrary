Book Review: The 5 AM Club

Title:

The 5 AM Club

Author:

Robin Sharma

Publisher

Harper Collins

Publication Year:

4 December 2018

Genre:

Self Help (personality development)

Language:

English

About the Author

This ground breaking book was penned down by very famous writer "Robin Sharma." He is a canadian writer, best known for his "Monk Who Sold His Ferrari" book series Sharma worked as a litigation lawyer until age 25, when he self-published "Megaliving" in 1994 a book on "stress management and spirituality." He is also a founder of the training company "Sharma Leadership International." He mostly works on self help generes. He is also one of the great motivational speakers. He also have "The Brandlaureate Brand Personality Award" and "Golden Gavel" award.

About the Book

In this groundbreaking book Sharma reveals the secret behind success of many successful people. Through this book readers can get lots of values to improve their personality. Through this book writer is taking the readers directely on the path of great success. We understand concepts like "life is to short to play with our small talents, do not live as you have 1000 years left, one who sweats more in training about bleeds less in war, obstacles are to test how seriously we want rewards, you can't win the game you don't play." We understand the importance of focus and energy and how can we increase it naturally from this book. We also understand the importance of time.

We started realising from this book that for success we should give atleast one hour to ourselves, we should invest our priceless time to get better form of our own. In this book the writer has highlighted the concept of 5 am club easily to readers writer also included some rules like 2x3 mindset rule, 90/90/1 rule, 50/10 methods in this marvalous book writer also first 50 minutes of morning as a victory periods.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Writer have used very simple, clear and straight forward language while composing this miraculas baok. He also has used some wisdoms and proverbs to impart easy understanding to readers and to minimize the content with deep meaning. This book has total 336 pages with 17 chapters.

My Opinion About the Book

I think this book is very helpful book in personality developement for everyone to develope or maintain their legacy to become master of their field this book will work as a key to success. I would like to suggest this book to everyone who wants to acheive something in thetr life

Moral

Those who can give respect to time, time gives respect to them.
