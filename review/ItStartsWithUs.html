Book Review: It Starts With Us

Title:

It Starts With Us

Author:

Colleen Hoover

Atria books

Publication Year:

18 Oct 2016

Genre:

Romantic (Emotional)

Language:

English (Original Language)

About the Author

"It Ends with us this very famous book was penned down by "Colleen Hoover." She is an American author who primarily writes novels in the romance & young adult fiction generes & also she sometimes venture into the psychological thirller. Many of her works were self-published before being picked up by publishing house. As of October 2022 Hoover has sold approximately 20 million books. She is named one of the 100 most influential people in the world by Time Magazine in 2023. Her first book "slammed was published in November 2011 and "Ugly Love" is also very famous book Of her.

About the Book

"It starts with us" this book is very perfect example of author's writing chops and her ability to weave together uplifting, romantic and somber plotliness. This book is a completing part of very famous novel "It Ends With Us." This book presents the past and present of iur protagonist and explores the difficultu of escaping abuse cycles and reveals the power of love and community in healing trauma. Story of this book includes protagonist's second chance romance with her childhood lover. We read through as they rekindle their love while navigating through coparenting with her abusive ex-husband. Through this novel readers will definately get aware about trust, understand, taking care are more impoortant than physical attraction for good life. At the end of this novel the confusion in the mind of our protagonist gets clear and she choosed the perfect person. This novel has very simple story but due to writers beautiful, skillful and emotional description and perfect presentation this novel has power to hold the readers with same excitement from first line to last word. This novel was nominated for best romance and named W.H.Smith's book of the year for 2022. This book is a perfect combination of love, care, romance, faith, understanding, thrill, arguments, and domestic voilence.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Writer have use very simple sweet and straight forward language while composing this novel. As this books basic language is American English writers has also used some authentic beautiful and descriptive words from ancient American English. This book is a short novel kind of fiction with 352 pages. It contains total 37 chapters.

My Opinion About the Book

I really like this book very much. I think this is really a fantanstic, mind-blowing book. Through this book readers can understand how to build relationships. I would like to suggest this book humbly to people over 16 because it also contains domestic voilance.
