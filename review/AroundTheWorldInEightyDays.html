About the Book

" Around The Word In Eighty Days" this impractical, imaginary and ground breaking novel is full of adventure and the excitement with which the readers come across and enjoy from beginning to the end. The Protagonist in the novel, accepts the challenge to go around the whole world in eighty days and in accomplishing this feat he goes through various lands and meets with diverse people and adventures. Thus the novel proceeds at a fast pace and there is always some excitement resulting from the various encounters. The beauty of the novel is that the writer takes the readers to a journey of many hair raising incidents and exciting adventures, thrilling yet beautiful places in the world. The most important feature of this mirraculous novel is "Time." It illustrates repeatedly that time is fickle, and either works for or against them. In many cases, time foils there plans, when they delay build up and ships and trains leave without them that sometimes land the characters in trouble. Throughout the entire trip and protagonist and his group encounter various obstacles standing in their way. These challenges allow them to use their quick thinking to come up with innovative solutions to even the most complicated of problems, relaying the message that no problem is unsolvable.
