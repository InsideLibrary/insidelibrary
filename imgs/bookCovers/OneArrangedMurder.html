Book Review: One Arranged Murder

Title:

One Arranged Murder

Author:

Chetan Bhagat

Publication:

Westland Publications

Publication Year:

2020

Genre:

Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Language:

English (Original)

About the Author

Chetan Bhagat, a well-known Indian author, is recognized for his contemporary writing that reflects the experiences of India’s urban youth. His novels typically blend romance, drama, and social issues, often with a humorous undertone. Bhagat, an IIT and IIM graduate, transitioned from a successful banking career to become a bestselling author. "One Arranged Murder" is his second attempt at the murder mystery genre, following the success of "The Girl in Room 105." With this book, Bhagat brings suspense and humor together while exploring themes of friendship, love, and family.

About the Book

"One Arranged Murder" is a mystery thriller set against the backdrop of a typical Indian arranged marriage. The story revolves around Keshav and Saurabh, two best friends who run a private detective agency. On the night of his fiancée Prerna’s karva chauth, Saurabh eagerly waits to break her fast, but she never arrives. Soon after, Prerna is found dead, apparently having fallen from the terrace. However, Keshav and Saurabh suspect foul play and begin investigating what they believe to be a murder. The novel combines elements of humor, suspense, and family drama as the duo navigate through a complex web of family secrets, love, and betrayal to uncover the truth behind Prerna’s death.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Chetan Bhagat’s writing in "One Arranged Murder" is light, engaging, and fast-paced. The language is simple and conversational, making the book accessible to a wide range of readers. Bhagat blends suspense with humor, maintaining a balance between the mystery and the light-hearted banter between Keshav and Saurabh. The characters are relatable, and the plot keeps readers hooked with its twists and revelations. The novel’s use of contemporary, everyday language makes it an easy read, while the blend of family drama and mystery adds emotional depth to the thriller. At 312 pages, the book moves at a brisk pace, maintaining suspense throughout.

My Opinion About the Book

"One Arranged Murder" is an entertaining and quick-paced thriller that offers a good mix of suspense, humor, and family drama. While the murder mystery itself is intriguing, the real charm of the book lies in the relationship between the two friends, Keshav and Saurabh. Bhagat manages to keep readers engaged with the plot twists and the humorous exchanges between the characters. Though the storyline might not be as complex as some seasoned mystery novels, it’s a fun and easy read, perfect for those looking for a light thriller with a touch of humor. Fans of Chetan Bhagat’s previous works will enjoy this novel for its relatable characters and engaging narrative style.
