Book Review: Learning How to Fly: Life Lessons for the Youth

Title:

Learning How to Fly: Life Lessons for the Youth

Author:

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Publication:

Universities Press

Publication Year:

2016

Genre:

Self-help, Inspirational

Language:

English (Original)

Number of Pages:

160

About the Author

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an eminent Indian scientist, educator, and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contributions to the country’s missile and space programs, he also became known as the "People's President" due to his approachable nature and commitment to public service. Kalam authored several inspiring books, most notably *Wings of Fire*. His work focused on empowering the youth, instilling a sense of vision, determination, and ethical leadership in them. His legacy continues to inspire generations of young minds.

About the Book

*Learning How to Fly: Life Lessons for the Youth* is a compilation of A.P.J. Abdul Kalam's speeches and thoughts aimed at inspiring young readers. Through this book, Kalam shares his vision for India's youth, emphasizing the importance of education, hard work, perseverance, and leadership. He offers practical advice on how to handle challenges, develop a strong character, and set lofty goals. With anecdotes from his own life and experiences, Kalam encourages readers to aim high and believe in their potential to achieve greatness.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Kalam's writing is simple, direct, and motivating. His approachable tone ensures that complex ideas are conveyed in an easy-to-understand manner. The book is structured into short, digestible lessons that make it accessible for readers of all ages. His use of personal stories and motivational anecdotes ensures that the book is both engaging and relatable. Kalam’s encouraging voice resonates throughout the pages, urging young readers to take action and dream big.

My Opinion About the Book

I found *Learning How to Fly: Life Lessons for the Youth* to be an inspiring and insightful read. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam’s passion for empowering young people is evident in every chapter. His emphasis on self-belief, hard work, and ethical leadership serves as a powerful message for readers. The book provides practical life lessons and is an excellent resource for anyone, particularly young readers, who are looking to cultivate a positive mindset and achieve their full potential. It is a motivational guide that leaves a lasting impact, encouraging readers to aim high and stay grounded in values.
