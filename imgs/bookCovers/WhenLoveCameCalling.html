Book Review: When Love Came Calling

Title:

When Love Came Calling

Author:

Preeti Shenoy

Publication:

Westland Publications

Publication Year:

2015

Genre:

Fiction, Romance

Language:

English (Original)

About the Author

Preeti Shenoy is a bestselling Indian author known for her compelling narratives that often explore themes of love, relationships, and personal growth. With multiple successful novels to her credit, Shenoy has become a prominent voice in contemporary Indian literature. Her writing draws inspiration from her life experiences, making her stories relatable and engaging for readers of all backgrounds.

About the Book

*When Love Came Calling* tells the story of a young woman who experiences the transformative power of love as she navigates her life’s challenges. The protagonist embarks on a journey of self-discovery, learning valuable lessons about love, friendship, and resilience. Shenoy's narrative intricately weaves moments of joy and sorrow, exploring how love can change our perspectives and ultimately lead us to find our true selves.

Language of the Book and Writing Style

Preeti Shenoy’s writing style is characterized by its simplicity and emotional depth, making the book accessible to a broad audience. Her conversational tone and engaging prose invite readers into the protagonist's world, while her vivid descriptions enhance the reading experience. The book is structured into manageable chapters, with relatable dialogue and compelling character arcs that resonate with readers, making it easy to get lost in the story.

My Opinion About the Book

I found *When Love Came Calling* to be an engaging and heartfelt exploration of love and personal growth. Preeti Shenoy’s ability to capture the nuances of relationships and the complexities of human emotions is impressive. The relatable characters and their journeys provide a reflective mirror for readers, prompting them to think about their own experiences with love. Although some plot elements may feel familiar, Shenoy’s unique storytelling and emotional authenticity make this book a worthwhile and enjoyable read for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of love and connection.
